01

How team members work

The People page (/people/) is organised into two parts: an Our Leaders section (Founder & Directors, shown as large image+bio rows that alternate sides), and four department grids (Business Development, Design Team, Project Management, Administration). Each person is an entry in a custom post type called ecec_team_member — add a new person and they appear in the right section automatically based on their Tier and Department.

The page sections are wired to three shortcodes: [ecec_team_leadership] for the leader rows, and [ecec_team_department dept="..."] for each department grid. You don't normally need to touch these — just add people and they slot in.

02

Adding a new team member

Open Team MembersFrom WP Admin sidebar → Team Members → Add New.
Enter the person's nameUse the title field at the top — this becomes their display name.
Pick a TierIn the Team Member Details box, choose Senior team for normal staff (appears in a department grid), Director for a department head (appears as a large image + bio row in the Our Leaders section), or Founder / Managing Director (appears at the top of Our Leaders).
Pick a Department (Senior team only)Choose one of: Business Development, Design Team, Project Management, or Administration. This decides which grid the person appears in. Ignored for Founder/Director (they go to Our Leaders instead).
Set the Featured Image (their portrait)Right sidebar → Featured Image → Set featured image. Use a square photo, ideally 800 × 800. Crop tightly.
Fill in Role and LocationsRole is the job title (e.g. "Senior MEP Engineer"). Locations are office cities, separated by | (e.g. "Dubai | Riyadh | Amman"). Locations only display on Founder/Director rows.
Write a Bio (Founder & Director only)Use the Bio textarea — about 100–150 words. This narrative appears next to the photo on the leadership row. Senior-team cards don't display the bio.
Click PublishThe person appears in the relevant section immediately.
✓
Photo specs:

Square crop, 800 × 800 minimum, JPG. Face roughly centred. The grid renders these at fluid widths up to 400px so anything smaller than 400px source will look soft on big screens.

Editing an existing person

Open the team listWP Admin → Team Members. You'll see all current entries.
Click their nameOpens the edit screen.
Update any fieldName, photo, role, locations.
Click UpdateLive on the People page immediately.
04

Reordering the grid

Each team member has a Menu Order field in the right sidebar of their edit screen. The grid sorts ascending: lower numbers appear first.

  1. Open each person you want to reorder.
  2. Set Menu Order to a number reflecting their position (e.g. CEO = 1, CTO = 2, Senior Engineer = 10).
  3. Click Update.
i
Power tip:

Use gaps of 10 (10, 20, 30...) so you can insert new people in between later without renumbering everyone. "Menu Order 25" slots between 20 and 30.

05

Removing someone

From the team list, hover their row and click Trash. They disappear from the live grid. To restore, go to the Trash tab and click Restore.

To permanently delete: open Trash, hover the row, click Delete Permanently. Their featured image stays in the media library — delete that separately if needed.

06

Page layout & placeholders

The People page is composed of six top-to-bottom sections, all wired automatically by shortcodes:

  1. Hero band — a full-width gray strip under the site header. Currently empty by design; can be filled with an image later by editing the section's background in Elementor.
  2. "OUR LEADERS" heading.
  3. Leader rows — [ecec_team_leadership]. Renders Founder & Director records as alternating full-width image + bio rows (photo on the left for the first leader, right for the second, etc.).
  4. Business Development — [ecec_team_department dept="business-development" columns="4" min="3"].
  5. Design Team — [ecec_team_department dept="design-team" columns="4" min="9"].
  6. Project Management — [ecec_team_department dept="project-management" columns="4" min="3"].
  7. Administration — [ecec_team_department dept="administration" columns="4" min="3"].

Placeholder cards

Each department grid has a min attribute. If you have fewer real members in a department than min, the grid pads with light-gray placeholder cards labelled "Name of employee / Position" so the section looks complete while you're recruiting. Once published members reach min, the placeholders disappear automatically.

To change a department's column count or padding count, edit the shortcode in Elementor. For example to make the Design Team grid 3-column with no placeholders:

  • [ecec_team_department dept="design-team" columns="3" min="0"]
07

FAQ

I added a team member but they don't show on the People page.

Check post status is Publish, not Draft. Also check the team list is not filtered to "Trash". Hard refresh the People page after publishing.

The portrait is squashed / stretched.

The card forces a square (1:1) aspect ratio. Upload a square crop — if your source isn't square, crop in WP Admin → Media → Edit image, or pre-crop with any image tool.

Can I add a bio or social links?

Bios are displayed for Founder & Director tiers — fill in the Bio field on their edit screen and it appears next to their photo on the Our Leaders section. Senior-team cards don't show bios on the department grid (kept compact by design). Social links aren't part of the current data model — ask the developer if you want them added.

I added a Senior team member but they don't show up.

Senior-tier members only appear in the department grid that matches their Department selection. Open their edit screen, pick a department, and click Update. If Department is blank, the person is published but invisible on the page.

How do I show role next to name on the live page?

Role IS shown by default on every card (under the photo). If yours isn't appearing, check that the Role meta field on the team member's edit screen isn't empty.
